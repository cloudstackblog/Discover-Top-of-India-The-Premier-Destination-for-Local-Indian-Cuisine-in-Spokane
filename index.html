Why Local Indian Cuisine is a Must-Try in Spokane

For many, the allure of Indian food lies in its complex and varied flavors. Spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, cardamom, and garam masala create a rich tapestry of taste. Combining these ingredients with slow cooking methods results in deep, satisfying flavors that have been perfected over centuries.

In addition to its rich flavors, Indian cuisine offers various health benefits. Many spices used in Indian cooking are known for their anti-inflammatory properties, digestive benefits, and ability to support overall well-being. For example, turmeric is praised for its antioxidant properties, while ginger is known to help with digestion and reduce nausea.

At Top of India, we strive to deliver all these benefits, ensuring that every bite is as nutritious as it is delicious.

The Perfect Pairing: Drinks and Desserts
